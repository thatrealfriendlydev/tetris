Party Bookmarklet

This script adds animations and music to the current page. It does not install anything on your computer.

[Drag to Bookmarks Bar]
How to install:
  1. Ensure your Bookmarks Bar is visible (Ctrl+Shift+B).
  2. Click and drag the link above into your Bookmarks Bar.
  3. Go to any website and click the bookmark to start the party.

Note: Works best in Chrome and Firefox. Make sure your volume is up!
